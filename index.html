Cobb County is a county in the U.S. state
of Georgia, located in the Atlanta metropolitan
area in the north central portion of the state.
As of 2020 Census, the population was 766,149.
It is the state's third most populous county, after
Fulton and Gwinnett counties. Its county seat is
Marietta; its largest city is Mableton.

Phoenix (/ˈfiːnɪks/ FEE-niks; Navajo: Hoozdo,
[xòːztò]; O'odham: S-ki:kigk; Spanish: Fénix
Walapai: Banyà:nyuwá) is the state capital and
most populous city of the U.S. state of Arizona,
with 1,608,139 residents as of 2020. It is the
fifth-most populous city in the United States and
the most populous state capital in the country.

Dubai (/duːˈbaɪ/, doo-BY; Arabic: دبي, romanized:
Dubayy, IPA: [dʊˈbajj], Gulf Arabic pronunciation:
[dəˈbaj]) is the most populous city in the United
Arab Emirates (UAE) and the capital of the
Emirate of Dubai, the most populated of the
country's seven emirates.
